# **App Name**: Sonic Canvas

## Core Features:

- Project Showcase: Display your music projects with detailed descriptions, including your role, software used (Logic Pro X), and notable achievements. Each project will have an individual page featuring embedded audio and screenshots of the mixing process. Showcase cover song productions, instrumental arrangements, recorded and mixed keyboard-based covers, created backing tracks and music arrangements
- Keyboard Performance Videos: Embed videos of your keyboard performances.
- Certification Verification: Showcase your Trinity College London certifications, including grades from Electronic Keyboard (Grades 1-4) and Drum Kit (Grade 1). Include digital copies of your certificates that can be viewed on the page.
- Interactive Bio: A dynamically updated biography section generated with AI that pulls relevant info from your resume, highlighting your experience as an aspiring sound engineer, your passion for music production using Logic Pro X, your skills in recording, mixing, and audio editing, and your live performance experience with 100+ programs.
- Contact Form: An easy-to-use form for Nottingham Trent University admissions to contact you directly.
- Skills Showcase: Showcase skills in Logic Pro X, recording and mixing workflow, audio editing, music theory & chord progressions, keyboard performance, drums (foundational), pitch recognition / tuning correction, and improvisation.
- Experience Highlights: Highlight your audio production experience with Logic Pro X, including recording, layering, editing, and mixing tracks, working with EQ, compression, reverb, automation, and creating cover versions and instrumental tracks.
- Live Performance Record: Detail your live performance experience, including roles as lead keyboard player and supporting drummer, and your experience managing live sound coordination with event organizers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#AFCBD5), provides a calm and professional ambiance.
- Background color: Very light blue (#ECF8FD), offers a soft backdrop enhancing content visibility.
- Accent color: Dark navy (#272838), highlights interactive elements with sophistication.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif for a clean, modern and readable experience.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif for a futuristic and interesting feel.
- Use minimalistic, line-style icons related to audio production and music to enhance user navigation and visual appeal.
- Employs a grid-based layout to ensure a clean, structured presentation. Use white space to draw focus to your projects.
- Use subtle transitions and loading animations to provide feedback and maintain user engagement without being distracting.
